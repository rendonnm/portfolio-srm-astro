---

I was always curious about how those boxes that seemed magical worked.  
As a child, I spent hours in front of the computer, installing things, **breaking others (accidentally 😅)**, and wondering how everything could work so well.

At some point I thought about studying graphic design, because I was always drawn to the visual — _art, the way of communicating without words_.  
But it was in software development where I found the best of both worlds.

I studied Computer Engineering, and in fullstack development I discovered that exact point between logic, structure, and creativity: a place where I can build purposeful interfaces and develop systems that truly work.

I like creating digital products that flow, that respond, that speak the user's language.

I work within the **JavaScript** ecosystem, with a focus on **TypeScript** for the frontend, building modern and accessible experiences.  
On the backend I develop robust APIs with **Python** and **Django REST Framework**.

I'm from **Medellín** 🇨🇴, a city that inspires me every day with its energy, its people, and its capacity to reinvent itself.  
I love living here, and if you haven't been yet, you should give yourself the chance to come.

Outside the keyboard, I find inspiration in chess, my city's rap scene, and everything that has structure, art, and strategy.

**That's also being fullstack.**
